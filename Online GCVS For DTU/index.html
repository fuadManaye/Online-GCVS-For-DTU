Educational programs offered

These are the list of programs offered at Debre Tabor University.

Degree program

Debre Tabor University offers degree programs for students from different countries. As a result, students will be assigned to different classes based on their 12th grade results. For more information, click on the Department section above to find out about the degree programs offered by Debre Tabor University.

Extension Program

Debre Tabor University offers an extension program for various students.This extension program is offered on weekends and is a great opportunity for students here in Debre Tabor. As a result, students can reschedule their lessons from Monday to Friday so that they can study for two days, Saturday and Sunday.

Summer program

Debre Tabor University offers summer programs for various students. This summer program is offered in the summer and employees of various positions spend their summer time in this program.As a result, this program is a great opportunity for a variety of staff and students as regular students leave the campus.
