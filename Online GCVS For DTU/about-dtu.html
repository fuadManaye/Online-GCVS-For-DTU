About DTU

We are one of the best universities in Ethiopia

Debre Tabor University is one of the leading universities in Ethiopia and has been offering various educational programs to students from different parts of the country for many years.

Debre Tabor University is located in South Gondar and enrolls more than 4,000 students every year. In addition, the university graduates students each year through a variety of educational programs.
